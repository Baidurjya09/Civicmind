## 🎨 VISUAL DESIGN (Why It Works)

### Color System:
- **Green** = Stable, good, normal
- **Yellow** = Warning, strained, important
- **Red** = Critical, danger, urgent
- **Orange** = Rebel, chaos, threat

### Typography:
- **Courier New** = Monospace (feels like system)
- **Uppercase labels** = Professional
- **Letter spacing** = Control room aesthetic

### Animations:
- **Pulse** = Critical states (attention grabbing)
- **Gradient** = Rebel alert (dramatic)
- **Smooth transitions** = Professional

### Dark Theme:
- Background: #0a0e1a (very dark blue)
- Cards: #1a1f2e (slightly lighter)
- Text: #e0e0e0 (light gray)
- Accents: Bright colors (pop against dark)

**Result:** Looks like a NASA control room or military command center
